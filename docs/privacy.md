# Privacy Policy — AtCoder Dark

**Last updated:** 2026-07-13

## Summary

AtCoder Dark is a browser extension that only changes how [AtCoder](https://atcoder.jp) looks (dark theme). It does **not** collect personal data, does **not** sell data, and does **not** send data to remote servers operated by this project.

## Data stored locally

The extension may store the following in your browser (`chrome.storage.sync` and/or `localStorage`), only on your device / browser sync account:

| Key | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| Theme on/off | Remember dark or light preference |
| Locale preference | UI language: Auto / English / 中文 |

This data never leaves the browser except through **Chrome’s own sync** if you have signed in to Chrome and enabled extension sync. The extension author cannot access that data.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| `storage` | Save theme and language settings |
| Host access to `atcoder.jp` | Inject theme CSS/JS only on AtCoder pages |

## What we do **not** do

- No analytics or tracking pixels
- No advertising
- No account login handled by the extension
- No reading of contest answers or submissions for upload
- No transmission of browsing history to third parties by this extension

## Third-party pages

AtCoder itself and its third-party scripts (e.g. analytics, Cloudflare) are outside this extension’s control. Their policies apply when you use the website.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the project’s GitHub repository or contact the maintainer via the store listing support email.

## Changes

Material changes to this policy will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date and the extension version notes.
